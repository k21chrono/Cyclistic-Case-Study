# Cyclistic: Members vs. Casual Riders

A case study analyzing how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic's bike-share service, aimed at informing a marketing campaign to convert casual riders into members.

📄 **[Read the full case study](./Cyclistic_Members_vs_Casual_Riders.md)**

## Highlights

- Analyzed 653,704 rides from May 2026 using DuckDB (SQL) and Metabase (visualization)
- Found casual riders take **75% longer trips** on average and show a **leisure-driven** usage pattern, vs. members' **commuter-driven** pattern
- Identified a specific lakefront/tourist station cluster where casual ridership dominates
- Delivered targeted marketing recommendations based on trip duration, timing, and geography
